Clubs

Soccer

Welcome to ATUS Women's Soccer Club, where passion, teamwork, and dedication come together to create a thrilling soccer experience like no other. Our club is a close-knit community of female soccer enthusiasts, committed to fostering talent, personal growth, and a deep love for the beautiful game. Here, we celebrate the diversity of our players and the unity that soccer brings. Whether you're an aspiring professional athlete, a seasoned player, or a novice eager to learn, we have a place for you. Our club provides a supportive environment where women of all skill levels can thrive, improve, and excel.

Basketball Ladies

ATU Sligo's name is a well known in college basketball in Ireland, we have won all Ireland championship In men's and women's. The men's team played in division 1 against the top teams right before COVID hit this year they are playing in Division 2.

Our goals as a club is to create an environment that feels like a family and also to compete against the top teams in Ireland. We pride ourselves on looking out for each other on the court and in the classroom.

In the past years we have had a number of American scholarship students on our teams. In 2018/19 season our scholars were Zack Yonda (D3 Swarthmore College) and Conor McClenaghan (D1 Marist College). In 2019/20 our Scholars were Brandon Berry (D3 Elizabethtown, Blue Jays) and Carson Long (D3 Guilford, Quakers).

Cricket

Welcome to our Cricket Club, where passion meets precision on the pitch! Our club is a tight-knit community of cricket enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're a seasoned player or a beginner looking to learn the art of the game, we provide a friendly and supportive environment for everyone.

We organize regular practice sessions, friendly matches, and tournaments to help our members hone their skills and showcase their talents. Our experienced coaching staff is dedicated to nurturing talent and instilling a love for the sport.

Come play with us and let's create unforgettable cricketing moments together!

Basketball (Mens)

Welcome to our Men's Basketball Club, where we're not just a team; we're a brotherhood united by our love for the game. Our club is a powerhouse of talent and passion, bringing together players of all levels to share in the thrill of the court.

Whether you're a seasoned baller with years of experience or just starting out on your basketball journey, you'll find a home here. We offer rigorous training, exhilarating matches, and a supportive community that pushes each member to reach their full potential.

Off the court, we're more than just teammates; we're friends who bond over a shared dedication to the sport. Join us to be part of a legacy of basketball excellence, where teamwork, sportsmanship, and the love of the game are at the core of everything we do.

Come dribble, shoot, and slam dunk with us as we aim for victory and create memorable basketball moments together!

Fencing

Welcome to our Fencing Club, where the ancient art of swordplay meets modern athleticism! We are a passionate group of fencers, from beginners to seasoned competitors, who share a love for this elegant and strategic sport.

Our club offers a supportive and inclusive environment, perfect for both those looking to try fencing for the first time and experienced fencers seeking to hone their skills. We provide expert coaching, regular training sessions, and opportunities to compete at various levels.

Beyond the foil, epee, and saber, our club fosters a sense of camaraderie and discipline. Join us to forge lasting friendships and explore the rich history and technique of fencing. Whether you dream of dueling like a musketeer or simply want a unique workout, our club is the perfect place to start your fencing journey.
